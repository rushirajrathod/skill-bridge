Role: Junior Machine Learning Engineer

About the Role:
We are looking for a Junior ML Engineer to help us build and deploy predictive models. You will work closely with our data science team to clean datasets, train models, and integrate them into our production environment.

Required Skills:
- Proficiency in Python and familiarity with ML libraries (Scikit-Learn, Pandas, NumPy).
- Hands-on experience with Deep Learning frameworks like PyTorch or TensorFlow.
- Understanding of SQL and NoSQL databases (PostgreSQL, MongoDB).
- Familiarity with version control using Git.
- Basic knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS or Google Cloud).
- Strong problem-solving skills and a passion for data-driven insights.

Bonus Points:
- Experience with Docker and containerization.
- Knowledge of MLOps principles.
